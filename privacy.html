Schmidt Scoreboard does not collect or sell personal information of any kind. It may collect Scoreboard usage information purely for internal use and feature selection, but it will never collect your wifi information or other private information.

BetterBlue Privacy and Usage Policy

BetterBlue does not collect or sell personal information of any kind. All user data, including vehicle credentials and preferences, are stored securely on your device and synchronized through iCloud using Apple's encrypted cloud storage.

BetterBlue communicates directly with Hyundai BlueLink and Kia Connect services using your existing vehicle subscription credentials. No vehicle data or commands are routed through third-party servers - all communications are direct between the app and your vehicle manufacturer's APIs.
