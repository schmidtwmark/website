Fidgets

Fidget Cube for Apple Watch

Use the tactile Fidget screens to relax and distract yourself. There are 6 unique screens:

  1. Spin the digital crown and feel the rotation
  2. Tilt your wrist to bounce a ball and collect goals
  3. Fiddle with a virtual joystick
  4. Toggle a switch
  5. Press a haptic button
  6. Breathe with the square breathing trainer to quiet your mind

Fidgets is built with SwiftUI and is fully open source--check out the source here
